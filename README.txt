Lost Control README

Couldn't find an addon that would help me, as a healer, to ensure my party members were aware of when I was silenced, feared, stunned or otherwise incapacitated and unable to heal. So I wrote this! 
It is mainly under development just for me and people I know, but others are of course welcome to use it - just beware that there may be small bugs, in which case do let me know and I'll see what I can do.
